Grace Fellowship    About the people of Grace

Grace Fellowship of Mountain Home is a body of believers in Jesus Christ who are joined together for the worship, service, prayer, fellowship, discipleship, evangelism and for the observance of the ordinances of baptism and the Lord's supper as set forth in the Bible.

 About our Church Family    Our History    Our Worship Service

Our small church comes together each Sunday to Worship, share and fellowship in God's Love.  We have several extended families that are stationed in all parts of the world and still consider this their Church home.  Come and enjoy the Love of Christ with us.
 

In December of 1993, Pastor Hurley returned to Mountain Home and began Calvary Chapel, which is now called Grace Fellowship. Starting with three couples, our church has grown to its present size.
 

Our worship service is a sacrifice offered up to God.  Using Contemporary praise and worship songs we sing, dance, clap and raise our hands unto our Lord and Savior as a living sacrifice (Romans 12:1).
